Add Funds to Child's Wallet (Manual MPESA)

How to Update Wallet via MPESA:
1. Go to M-PESA on your phone.
2. Select Lipa na M-PESA.
3. Choose Paybill.
4. Enter Business Number: 174379
5. Enter Account Number: Input Child's Name
6. Enter the Amount.
7. Enter your M-PESA PIN and confirm.
8. After payment, fill the form below with your details and transaction code.
