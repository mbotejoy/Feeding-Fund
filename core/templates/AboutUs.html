About Us

Nourished by Joy and Grace is a non-profit initiative dedicated to providing healthy school meals to children in underserved communities. Our mission is to ensure that every child can learn and grow without the barrier of hunger.

Founded by a passionate group of volunteers, we believe that every meal served is a step towards a brighter future. With the support of donors, schools, and community members, we have delivered over 10,000 meals and counting.
